The UNFASHIONED Story

Unfashioned, born on the streets of Aarhus, Denmark, is more than just a streetwear brand; it's a representation of the vibrant and eclectic spirit of the city. In the heart of Aarhus, where modern urban culture meets timeless traditions, Unfashioned has carved its niche.

Inspired by the dynamic street culture and the unique blend of historic and contemporary elements in Aarhus, Unfashioned embodies a style that transcends trends. Our clothing is not just a fashion statement; it's a reflection of the diverse and evolving stories written on the streets of this beautiful Danish city.


At Unfashioned, we believe in breaking away from conventional fashion norms. Our designs draw inspiration from the city's ever-changing landscapes, from the picturesque streets of Old Town to the modern architecture of Dokk1. Each piece tells a story of urban exploration, creativity, and the people who make Aarhus a vibrant and inclusive community.

We take pride in our commitment to sustainability and quality craftsmanship. Every garment is carefully curated, ensuring that it not only captures the essence of Aarhus but also stands the test of time. Unfashioned is more than clothing; it's a narrative woven into the fabric of Aarhus' streets, embracing the past, present, and future.

Join us on this journey as we redefine streetwear, one story at a time. Unfashioned: Where Aarhus meets style, and tradition meets the avant-garde.
